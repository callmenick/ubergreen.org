The tropical islands of Trinidad & Tobago, nestled in the southern Caribbean, are where Trinitario cacao has its origins. Since the 19th century, farmers here have been growing some of the highest quality cacao in the world, regularly winning awards for quality and flavour. Ubergreen's mission is to support small-scale Caribbean producers who are focused on flavour, quality, and fair trade over mass production.
